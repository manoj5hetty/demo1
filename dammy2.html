Learn from industry experts with a focus on real-world skills. Choose your preferred domain and kickstart your career with Winger IT Solutions. We offer a comprehensive internship program tailored for students and professionals looking to gain hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies.

Why Winger IT Solutions Internship?

Welcome to this exciting Internship program hosted by Winger IT Solutions. We are a 15-year-old company based in India, Bangalore specializing in IT infrastructure and software solutions. Our expertise spans across various technologies, including Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning/Data Science, Full Stack Web/Software Development, and Embedded Systems and IoT.

During this internship program, you will have the opportunity to learn about Futuristic Technologies such as Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Full Stack Web Development on the software side. On the hardware side, the program will cover Embedded Systems, IoT, and Robotics.

The program is meticulously designed by industry experts to offer you exposure to industry standard practices. Regardless of your engineering branch, you will have the chance to learn these technologies from scratch, helping you develop essential working skills, enhancing your competitiveness in the job market.
